Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our academy, courses, and admissions process.

Do I need prior coding experience? +

Not at all! Our foundational courses, like Web Fundamentals, are specifically designed for complete beginners who have never written a line of code. We start from the absolute basics and guide you up to advanced concepts.

What kind of computer or software do I need? +

Any basic laptop (Windows, Mac, or Linux) released in the last 5 to 7 years will work perfectly. You don't need a massive gaming PC to write code. All the software we use (like VS Code, Node.js, and Git) is completely free to download.

What is the difference between Self-Paced and Bootcamp? +

The Self-Paced tier gives you access to all our video curriculum and automated grading, allowing you to learn on your own schedule. The Intensive Bootcamp includes live instructor-led Zoom classes, 1-on-1 mentorship with our Directors, code reviews, and career placement support.

Do you offer job placement support? +

Yes. Students enrolled in our Intensive Bootcamp receive full career support. This includes resume and LinkedIn reviews, portfolio building, mock technical interviews, and direct referrals to our hiring partners.

Will I receive a certificate? +

Yes! Upon successful completion of your final capstone project, you will receive a verifiable digital certificate from {CODING HOME} that you can add to your LinkedIn profile and resume.

We're here to help. Send us a message and our team will get back to you within 24 hours.
